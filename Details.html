How It Spreads?

The virus can spread from an infected person’s mouth or nose in small liquid particles when they cough, sneeze, speak, sing or breathe. These particles range from larger respiratory droplets to smaller aerosols.

Read more

Symptoms of COVID-19

The symptoms of COVID-19 infection are similar to that of an acute respiratory infection or pneumonia. These symptoms include fever, cough, runny nose, sore throat, loss of taste or smell.

Read more

Treatment for COVID-19

It’s often similar to treating influenza (the flu) or cold symptoms. For people at risk for severe disease, their doctor may prescribe additional treatments such as monoclonal antibodies or oral medication.
